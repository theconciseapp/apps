True Attendance — Smart attendance for Nigerian primary & secondary schools

A product of Oshobby Enterprise, True Attendance helps teachers record class attendance quickly and generates accurate, concise Daily, Monthly, Termly, Session and Yearly attendance reports.

What is a School Key?

A School key is a unique identifier (simply a username) that you create when you register your school on our platform. For example, if the name of your school is "Amazing Grace International School of Excellence", you can create a nice school key like "Amazing" or "AGISE". This School key will be used by your teachers along with their staff keys(auto-generated) to login on True Attendance app to manage class attendance and submit online anytime, anyday.

Why use a School Key?

While individual teachers can use True Attendance app without a school key, but with a School Key, the experience becomes center-based and connected, unlocks full school-wide administration and reporting features.

Unified administration
With a school key, all teachers share the same or uniform configurations such as School name, address, school term/session, resumption & closing time, logo e.t.c set by the school admin from the dashboard. The school admin sets up the school once on the dashboard, add Staffs (Teachers), create classes and assign classes to each staff.
Centralized reporting
Teachers submit daily attendance directly to the dashboard, allowing the school admin to view, manage, and print reports with ease.
Parent access
Parents can view their child’s attendance daily, ensuring peace of mind that their children are present in school each day.

Tip: Use a School Key if you want connected teachers, clear reports, and daily transparency for parents and administrators.
